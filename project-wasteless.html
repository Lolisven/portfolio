Problem

Food waste is a significant global problem, with a third of all produced food going to waste each year. People often forget about their food inventory, leading to food spoilage and environmental issues. An app that tracks expiration dates and sends reminders can be a valuable solution to address this issue.

Goals

The goal of the Wasteless app is to reduce food waste and save users money by tracking expiration dates, providing reminders, and helping users make informed decisions about their food inventory.

Solutions
Research

I conducted a qualitative user research study with 6 participants to understand their perceptions and experiences with an app that tracks food expiration dates and provides recipe suggestions. Participants were generally positive about the app's concept. Overall, the research provided valuable insights into the app's potential strengths and weaknesses, and highlighted opportunities for further development and improvement.

Interviews

I conducted a series of interviews with individuals to gather insights about their food waste habits and potential interest in using an app to track food expiration dates. Many participants expressed frustration with throwing away food that had gone bad, and several mentioned using a paper calendar or simply guessing when items might expire. Most participants expressed interest in using an app that could help them keep track of their food expiration dates, with features like barcode scanning, recipe suggestions, and customizable reminders. However, some participants also mentioned concerns about the effort required to input items into the app, as well as the cost and potential difficulty in remembering to use it regularly.

Insights

Based on the results of the study, it is clear that there is a demand for an app that tracks food expiration dates and provides helpful features to minimize food waste. Participants generally expressed interest in using such an app, with many highlighting the potential benefits of receiving recipe recommendations and using a shopping list feature. However, some users cited barriers to use such as the effort required to log food items and the need for automatic reminders. Additionally, participants expressed a desire for features such as barcode scanning and categorization of food items to simplify the tracking process. Overall, the insights suggest that an app with clear and customizable features that cater to user needs and preferences could be successful in reducing food waste and simplifying meal planning.

Opportunities

Based on the insights gathered from the user research, there are several opportunities for an app that tracks food expiration dates and provides recipe suggestions:

  1. Simplifying the tracking process: Users expressed a desire for features such as barcode scanning and categorization of food items to make it easier to track and manage expiration dates.
  2. Providing customizable reminders: Users had varying preferences for the frequency of reminders, suggesting an opportunity for the app to provide options for reminder frequency and customization.
  3. Enhancing recipe recommendations: While users were generally positive about the recipe feature, some expressed concerns about the quality of recommendations. This suggests an opportunity to improve the recommendation algorithm and provide more personalized and accurate suggestions.

Overall, these opportunities suggest that there is significant potential for an app that can effectively address user pain points and provide valuable features to minimize food waste and simplify meal planning.

Ideation

After conducting user research and creating user personas, I have identified an opportunity to create an app that addresses the pain points of food waste due to expiration dates. The app will include features such as a shopping list, recipe suggestions based on food items that are about to expire, and the ability for family members to share one inventory. This will not only help users reduce food waste but also simplify the process of grocery shopping and meal planning. My goal is to provide an intuitive and user-friendly experience that seamlessly integrates into the usersâ€™ daily routine. I will prioritize a clean and visually appealing design, as well as functionality and ease of use.
