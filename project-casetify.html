Problem

Casetify's home page was already good, but it had not received any refreshment in a while. As a designer, I saw an opportunity to improve the website's user experience and visual design. I wanted to take advantage of the latest design trends and technologies to create a more engaging and modern website that would appeal to Casetify's target audience.

Goals

The goal of the redesign was to improve the user flow and make the design more appealing. I wanted to create a more intuitive experience for our users, making it easier for them to find and purchase the products they need. By updating the visual design, I also aimed to create a more modern and engaging look and feel for the website. Overall, I wanted to create a better user experience that would increase engagement.

Research

To start, I researched the brand, mission, vision, and target audience to gain a deeper understanding of the company's values and goals. Then, I analyzed the existing homepage and studied the competition to identify opportunities for improvement and unique features.

User research was a critical part of the process, as it helped me gather feedback and insights from Casetify's target audience. I used wireframes and prototypes to iteratively design and refine the layout, typography, color scheme, imagery, and overall user experience of the homepage. To create a cohesive visual identity, I developed a design that aligned with Casetify's brand guidelines.

Target Group

Casetify's target group is primarily young, tech-savvy, and fashion-conscious individuals who are looking for unique and personalized accessories for their mobile devices. This group is typically made up of millennials and Generation Z, who are active on social media and value self-expression and individuality.

Casetify's products are designed to appeal to this group by offering a wide range of customizable designs, trendy patterns, and stylish collaborations with popular brands and artists. Additionally, Casetify's focus on sustainability and eco-friendliness may also appeal to this target group, who are increasingly interested in ethical and sustainable consumer products.

Color Scheme

Casetify's website primarily uses a bold and playful color scheme, featuring a mix of bright, eye-catching colors.

The dominant colors used on Casetify's website are actually white (#FFFFFF) and black (#000000). These two colors create a clean and modern look that is consistent throughout the website.

While there are certainly accent colors used on the website, such as red, blue, and green, these colors are used more sparingly and primarily to draw attention to specific elements, such as buttons, important information and links.

Additionally, Casetify features a wide range of colorful product images on their website, which further reinforces the brand's fun and playful aesthetic. Overall, the use of bold and bright colors helps to create a visually engaging and memorable experience for users.

Layout

When creating the layout for my project, I started by defining my goals and objectives for the page. I wanted to increase engagement and build brand awareness, so I identified the key content areas that would help me achieve these goals, including promotions, perks, important items, latest arrivals, customer opinions, community, and a newsletter subscription form. Next, I worked on arranging these elements in a visually appealing and easy-to-navigate way.

Design Style

When designing my project, I wanted to create a style that was modern, sleek, and visually appealing. I focused on using a minimalist design approach, with clean lines and a simple color palette to make the content stand out. I also incorporated bold typography to add visual interest and hierarchy to the layout. To create a cohesive look and feel, I used consistent branding elements throughout the design, including typography and imagery. By paying attention to the details and focusing on a modern and minimalist style, I was able to create a visually compelling design that effectively communicated the brand message.
